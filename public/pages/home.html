Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

Do I need prior programming experience?

No! We offer courses for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced developers. Our beginner courses start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to more complex concepts.

How long do I have access to the courses?

Once you enroll in a course, you have lifetime access to the content, including any future updates. You can learn at your own pace and revisit the material whenever you need.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

Most beginner courses have no prerequisites. For intermediate and advanced courses, we clearly list any required knowledge so you can choose the right course for your skill level.

Do you offer certificates?

Yes! Upon completion of any course, you'll receive a certificate that you can share on LinkedIn and include in your resume or portfolio.

What if I need help during the course?

We have a dedicated community forum where you can ask questions and get help from both instructors and fellow students. Many courses also include live Q&A sessions.
